Home & Family Finance Manager Help

Local guidance for setting up and managing a family finance case.

Start here

Use the green Start Finance Setup or Continue Setup button to walk through the main setup process. The wizard creates the case, household, income, bills, allowance and savings, assets, debts, and documents using the same records shown in the normal pages.

Avoid double-counting

Money should live in one place. If a household member contributes money, link that person to an Income Source instead of typing the same amount twice.

Bills vs Debts

Bills / Spending are recurring payments that affect the monthly picture. Debts track obligations and can link to a bill when there is a monthly payment.

Assets

Assets are things owned, controlled, or managed, like vehicles, property, bank accounts, or valuable items. Related bills and income should be linked, not duplicated.

Documents

Documents can be tagged with comma-separated keywords and flagged as important so they appear on the Dashboard.

Common questions

What counts as a household contribution?

A household contribution is money physically transferred to the head of household to help pay bills. Do not enter bills the person directly pays and controls as a contribution, or the app may count the same money twice.

What does “Who Pays This?” mean?

It means who actually sends the payment. If someone other than the case person pays it, it can be tracked without reducing the primary person’s remaining money.
